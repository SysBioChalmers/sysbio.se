ACCOMMODATION

Participants are kindly asked to arrange their own accommodation. There are several hotels in the instant vicinity of the venue. Please note that Tübingen is very popular with tourists in December, so you are advised to book early.

TRAVEL

Tübingen is easily reachable by car, train or bus and the venue is in walking distance from the Tübingen train/bus station (Tübingen Omnibusbahnhof or Tübingen Hbf). The closest airport is Stuttgart (STR) and it is well connected with the major European hubs. From the airport take the bus 828 to Tübingen (ca. 1h, 7 EUR), or taxi (ca. 30min, 70 EUR). International participants should enquire about visa regulations to enter Germany (for example at http://www.germany-visa.org/).
