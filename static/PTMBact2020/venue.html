VENUE

The meeting and conference dinner will take place at the Scandic Copenhagen Hotel, Vester Søgade 6, 1601 Copenhagen V.


Photo from the hotel website

This centrally located, contemporary hotel offers impressive views of the Lakes and Copenhagen skyline. The hotel has an underground car parking and is situated just a few minutes’ walk from the Central Station and Vesterport Station – the main public transport hubs in the city. Tivoli Gardens and all downtown attraction od Copenhagen are within a walking distance from the hotel.
